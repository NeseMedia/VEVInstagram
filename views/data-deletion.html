Data Deletion Instructions

Your Privacy Matters to Us

ðŸ“Š Important Notice

We do not store any personal data permanently. All Instagram profile information is fetched in real-time and is not saved in our databases.

Understanding Our Data Handling

Instagram Widget for Vev operates on a zero-storage principle:

  • No Personal Data Storage: We don't maintain databases with user information
  • Real-time Data Fetching: Instagram data is retrieved only when requested and immediately transmitted
  • No User Accounts: Our service doesn't require user registration or login
  • Temporary Caching: Any caching is temporary for performance and automatically expires

What Data Might Exist

The only data that might temporarily exist includes:

  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs that may contain IP addresses and request information (automatically deleted after 30 days)
  • Cache Data: Temporary performance cache that expires automatically within 15 minutes
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics without personal identifiers

How to Request Data Deletion

Even though we don't store personal data, we respect your right to ensure your information is deleted. To request data deletion:

Method 1: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@nesemedia.no with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your Instagram username (if applicable)
  • Any specific concerns about data
  • Approximate dates of service usage

Response Time: Within 48 hours

Method 2: Automated Request

Fill out our data deletion form:

Instagram Account Data

If you want to delete data from Instagram itself:

  1. Go to your Instagram Settings
  2. Navigate to "Privacy and Security"
  3. Select "View Account Data" or "Download Data"
  4. Follow Instagram's data deletion process

Note: We cannot delete data from Instagram on your behalf. We only handle data requests related to our service.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have (if any)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to data processing

Your Rights Under CCPA

If you're a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Confirmation Process

When you submit a deletion request:

  1. We'll acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  2. We'll verify your identity if necessary
  3. We'll search for any temporary data
  4. We'll confirm deletion completion within 30 days
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email

Third-Party Data
